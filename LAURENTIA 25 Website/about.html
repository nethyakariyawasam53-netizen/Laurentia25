About Laurentia '25

Laurentia '25 is our school's annual get-together event, bringing together students, teachers, and alumni for a day full of fun, memories, and celebration.

Organized by the 25 batch, our goal is to create a warm and memorable experience for everyone involved.

Vision

We aim to strengthen friendships, celebrate our school journey, and give back to the school community by showcasing creativity and teamwork.
